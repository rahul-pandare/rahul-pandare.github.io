Worked under the guidance of prof. P.K. Ghosh from the Chemical engineering department at ICT and alongside his post-doctoral student Dr. Lokeshkumar Ramteke to tackle an industrial problem:
 Developed a chemical process to recycle dimethyl-glyoxime (DMG), an expensive reagent, used in removal of nickel impurities from industrial zinc sulfate solutions.
 Optimized the above chemical system to increase reaction yields and decrease operating costs by approximately 12% each.
 Provided consultations to Rubamin Ltd., a zinc product manufacturing company based in Vadodara, India, to reduce nickel impurities in their product stream and thus improve product quality.
