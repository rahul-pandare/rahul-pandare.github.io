Did Homepaper on Graphene synthesis and application of Graphene derivatives on Textiles under Prof. Ravindra Kale, Dept. of Fibres & Textile Processing Technology, ICT - Mumbai and Prof. K.V. Marathe, Dept. of Chemical Engineering, ICT – Mumbai.
 Synthesized Chlorographene and studied the application of Chlorographene on Polyester fabric which yields electrical conduction properties.
 Performed an exhaustive literature survey for selecting the process route and gathering the essential data.
 Estimated the rough economics of the selected process system to check the feasibility of its scale-up.
