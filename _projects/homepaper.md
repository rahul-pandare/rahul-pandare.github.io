Completed homepaper on _Graphene synthesis and application of Graphene derivatives on Textiles_ under Prof. Ravindra Kale, Dept. of Fibres & Textile Processing Technology, ICT - Mumbai and Prof. K.V. Marathe, Dept. of Chemical Engineering, ICT – Mumbai.</br>
* Synthesized Chlorographene and studied the application of Chlorographene on Polyester fabric which yields electrical conduction properties. </br>
* Performed an exhaustive literature survey for selecting the process route and gathering the essential data. </br>
* Estimated the rough economics of the selected process system to check the feasibility of its scale-up.
