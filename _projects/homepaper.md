Completed homepaper on _Graphene synthesis and application of Graphene derivatives on Textiles_ under Prof. Ravindra Kale, Dept. of Fibres & Textile Processing Technology, ICT - Mumbai and Prof. K.V. Marathe, Dept. of Chemical Engineering, ICT – Mumbai.</br></br>
In this project we studied the application of Chlorographene on Polyester fabric to yield conduction properties. It involved a three stage synthesis process. An exhaustive literature survey was an important part of selecting the process route and gathering the essential data. The process route consisted of three primary stages which are as follows:
- Synthesis of Graphene/ Graphene Oxide from Graphite Powder.
- Possible Modifications for Graphene derivatization.
- Mechanism for application of Graphene Derivatives on Polyester Fabric.

After deciding the synthesis route we performed the scale up and industrial feasibility calculations like equipment sizing, mass and energy balance calculations, Financial analysis and HAZOP studies.
