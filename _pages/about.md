Research Interests

My research interests include *```Catalysis```*, *```Process Development & Optimization```*, *```Sustainable Energy & Modified Materials```*. My research work includes process intentsification by reagent recovery, which I did under the guidance of [Prof. P. K. Ghosh](https://www.ictmumbai.edu.in/emp_profiledetail.aspx?nDeptID=caeks).
